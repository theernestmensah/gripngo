GreatKey Educational Consult

Empowering learners through a comprehensive digital platform for tuition and homeschooling.

The Challenge

GreatKey Educational Consult offers a wide range of services, from home tuition to adult education. However, their previous online presence didn't effectively communicate the breadth of their offerings, making it difficult for parents and students to find the specific programs they needed.

  • Unclear service categorization
  • Difficulty in booking home tuition
  • Limited visibility for vacation classes
  • Lack of digital resources for homeschooling

Our Solution

We developed a structured, informative website that clearly segments their diverse services. Features include a dedicated section for "Home Tuition" with tutor profiles, an easy inquiry form for vacation classes, and a resource hub for homeschooling parents.

  • Clear, segmented service pages
  • Simple inquiry and registration forms
  • Mobile-friendly resource access
  • SEO optimized for educational keywords

The Results

200% Enrollment Increase
50+ New Tutors
98% Parent Satisfaction
National Service Reach

GreatKey Educational Consult has seen a surge in inquiries and enrollments. The new platform has streamlined their administrative process, allowing them to focus on what matters most: delivering quality education.

E-commerce Fashion

BGBrand (House of BG)

Creating an immersive, editorial digital showroom for a premium fashion house.

The Challenge

House of BG needed a digital presence that matched the elegance and exclusivity of their physical garments. Their previous platform failed to showcase the intricate details of their designs and lacked the storytelling element essential for a luxury brand.

  • Lack of high-quality visual representation
  • Disconnected brand storytelling
  • Navigation that didn't support browsing collections
  • Limited mobile engagement

Our Solution

We designed a "Digital Showroom" experience. The site focuses on high-resolution imagery and minimalist design to let the clothes speak for themselves. We implemented smooth transitions and a curated "Lookbook" feature to guide users through the latest collections.

  • High-fidelity image galleries with zoom
  • Editorial-style layout for collections
  • Seamless mobile browsing experience
  • Integrated social media feeds for live updates

The Results

3x Online Engagement
Global Audience Reach
500+ Looks Showcased
4.9/5 Brand Perception

BGBrand's new website has become a central hub for fashion enthusiasts. The immersive design has significantly increased time-on-site and established House of BG as a forward-thinking player in the digital fashion space.
