The Problem

Traditional Cups Are Failing You

Cardboard sleeves slip, tear, and end up in landfills. Hot drinks burn fingers. The solution was always obvious — nobody built it. Until now.

Sleeve Slip

Cardboard sleeves slide off at the worst moments — spilling hot drinks and wasting paper in the process.

Burned Hands

Thin walls and no grip means customers burn their palms — leading to dropped cups and poor brand experience.

Wasteful Design

Sleeves are a separate production step — extra materials, extra cost, extra waste. A structural inefficiency baked in.

Enter Grip & Go

Built-In Handle

The handle is an integral part of the cup structure — zero slip, zero separate components, maximum comfort.

Cool-Touch Grip

Double-walled construction around the handle keeps your hand comfortable even with boiling beverages inside.

One-Piece Eco Design

Made from certified compostable materials in a single manufacturing pass — less waste, lower cost, greener footprint.
