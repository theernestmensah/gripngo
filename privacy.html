Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

At BrAbel, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (brabel.solutions) or engage our web design, development, and digital services. Please read it carefully. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described herein.

1 Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words whose initial letter is capitalised have specific meanings defined below. These definitions apply whether the terms appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • Company (referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our") means BrAbel Studios, a web design and development studio operating from Ghana and serving clients worldwide.
  • Website refers to the BrAbel website, accessible at brabel.solutions.
  • Service refers to the Website and all professional services we provide, including web design, web development, e-commerce solutions, and website maintenance.
  • You means the individual accessing our Website or Services, or the company or legal entity on whose behalf that individual is acting.
  • Personal Data means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Usage Data means data collected automatically through your use of the Website (e.g., page visit duration, IP address).
  • Cookies are small files placed on your device by a website that store details of your browsing activity.
  • Service Provider means any third-party individual or company that processes data on our behalf to help us deliver our Services.
  • Country refers to: Republic of Ghana.

2 Data We Collect

Personal Data

When you contact us, request a quote, or engage our services, we may ask you to provide personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business or company name
  • Project details and requirements
  • General location (city / country)

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you visit our Website. It may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, time spent on each page, and other diagnostic data. When you access our Website via a mobile device, we may also collect your mobile device type, unique ID, and mobile operating system.

Tracking Technologies & Cookies

We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our Website and store certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all Cookies or to alert you when a Cookie is being sent. However, some parts of our Website may not function properly without Cookies.

Cookie Type Duration Purpose
Necessary / Essential Session Essential for basic website functionality and security. Cannot be disabled.
Cookie Acceptance Persistent Records whether you have accepted our cookie policy.
Functionality Persistent Remembers your preferences (e.g., language, settings) for a personalised experience.
Analytics Persistent Helps us understand how visitors interact with our Website so we can improve it (e.g., Google Analytics).

3 How We Use Your Data

BrAbel uses Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our Services — including monitoring usage and ensuring functionality.
  • Responding to your enquiries — to contact you by email, telephone, or other means regarding your project requests or questions.
  • Managing our client relationship — processing proposals, agreements, and project communications.
  • Project delivery — using your provided content, materials, and preferences to execute the agreed scope of work.
  • Marketing communications — sending updates, offers, or news about our services that may interest you. You may opt out at any time.
  • Improving our Website — analysing usage trends to enhance design, performance, and user experience.
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable laws.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before this occurs.

Sharing Your Data

We may share your personal information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers — third parties who assist us in delivering our services (e.g., hosting, email delivery, analytics). They are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • With business partners — only where necessary to fulfil a service you have requested.
  • With your consent — for any purpose you have explicitly approved.
  • For legal reasons — when required by law, court order, or government authority.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4 Retention of Your Data

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Client project data is typically retained for a period of five (5) years after project completion unless a longer retention period is required by law. Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period, unless used to strengthen Website security or improve functionality.

5 Transfer of Your Data

Your information may be processed at our operating location in Ghana and transferred to — and maintained on — computers located in other jurisdictions where privacy laws may differ. Where such transfers occur, we take all reasonably necessary steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy. By submitting your information to us, you consent to this transfer.

6 Your Rights & Deleting Your Data

You have the following rights with respect to your Personal Data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it.
  • Objection — object to our processing of your personal data for marketing purposes at any time.
  • Portability — request that we provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@brabel.solutions. We will respond to your request within 30 days. Please note that we may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7 Disclosure of Your Data

Business Transactions

If BrAbel is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your Personal Data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your data is transferred and is subject to a different privacy policy.

Law Enforcement

Under certain circumstances, we may be required to disclose your Personal Data if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court, the Ghana Police Service, or a government agency).

Other Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of BrAbel
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with our Services
  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public
  • Protect against legal liability

8 Security of Your Data

The security of your Personal Data is important to us. We implement technically and organisationally appropriate security measures — including encryption, access controls, and secure communication channels — to protect your data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You are also responsible for keeping your own credentials and communications secure.

9 Children's Privacy

Our Website and Services are not directed to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from persons under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us immediately at hello@brabel.solutions and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems promptly.

10 Links to Other Websites

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit.

11 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. For material changes, we may also send a notice to the email address you have provided us. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes become effective when posted on this page.
